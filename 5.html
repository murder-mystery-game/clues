FINAL 1 177896
The Full Story
Vincent is a member of the Evercroft family. His mother, Tessa Evercroft (family tree), had been struggling to get the money owed to her after the death of her husband. She did not receive any help from anyone in the Evercroft family during this time.

Vincent felt that she deserved better and had also been cheated out of the manor that she should have inherited before her father lost it in a bet. After the death of Edward Evercroft, he hatched a plan to try to ingratiate himself into Samuel Evercroft's life and try get some money out of him.

When Samuel started getting sick, Vincent hoped that he might inherit the manor in Samuel's will. However, Samuel would only pass the manor down to his sister.

At this point, Vincent brought his sister Catherine Hargrove into the mix. She posed as Eleanor Evercroft to try and collect the inheritance.

Vincent was the only person that Samuel spoke to about Eleanor, so he had lots of information to pass on to her. However, he also passed on incorrect information from Samuel - like her birthday.

The plan was working well, and Samuel truly believed that Eleanor was his sister. 

Dr. Amarist was trying to keep Samuel in bed and away from the rooms of the house, so that he could explore without needing to break in at night. Samuel had heard footsteps downstairs many times but no one believed him.

Dr. Amarist had used keys he had copied to enter the manors of many of his patients, in search of a hidden treasure trove of art. He had no intention of killing Samuel. The poison was administered through a special tea that Dr. Amarist brought with him every week. Along with weakness, the poison also made Samuel extremely paranoid.

This paranoia made him suspicious of everyone. It was for this reason that he began investigating who might be interested in his manor.

On the day of his death, he read a newspaper article about Vincent Hargrove's mother and immediately realized that Vincent was a member of his family. He confronted Vincent in the privacy of his study that night. Vincent attacked him with a candlestick, and in his weakened state, he succumbed to the injury.
